About Me

I am an AI/ML research scientist, specializing in the development of trustworthy and reliable systems at scale. I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from EPFL (Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne) under the supervision of Prof. Michael Unser, and hold dual B.Sc. degrees in Electrical Engineering and Pure Mathematics from Sharif University of Technology (SUT), Tehran.


Awards & Grants

  • Swiss National Science Foundation Postdoc Fellowship for the proposal "Learning with Principles: Theory and Methods", 2021.
  • Best Student Paper Award, International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP), 2019
  • Gold Medalist, National Mathematics Olympiad, Iran, 2011

Personal Interests

With over six years of experience playing the Setar, I have completed formal training in Persian classical music, mastering its rich and intricate repertoire known as the Radif.

Cinema

I am a passionate cinephile and have been deeply engaged with cinema throughout adulthood.
